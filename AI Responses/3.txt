-----What did akwey do and how well did he do it?
qwen: "Trramä ayuvanìri makto Akwey nìyewla ha snaytx" translates to:"Akwey did it well and successfully."
llama:"Akwey enjoyed playing the game very much and succeeded at it." / Or, more idiomatically: "Akwey had a blast playing the game and did an excellent job!"

solution: Akwey rode disappointingly in yesterday’s games so he lost.
